This time you will be viewing images some of which you have seen before and some which you have not seen. You will view each picture for 5 seconds and will indicate, by clicking the remember, know or no buttons on the screen, if you have seen this image before in the initial image list.

Following this, you will have to rate the picture viewed on two scales; happy versus unhappy and calm versus excited.

This should last a maximum of 20 minutes.

You will be presented with three options for every image- know, remember and no




When you are ready to begin press Start
